About Slay by Rhy

At Slay by Rhy, we believe that confidence begins at your fingertips. Our salon was founded with the vision of offering not just stylish nails, but a self-care experience that uplifts your mood and empowers your inner beauty.

Located in the heart of San Jose del Monte, Bulacan, our studio welcomes clients into a warm, cozy space where creativity, cleanliness, and care come first. Whether you're here for a quick polish or an all-out nail transformation, our mission is to make you feel beautiful, bold, and completely slayed.

Every nail tells a story—and we’re here to help you write yours in color, art, and shine.
